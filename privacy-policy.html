Privacy Policy

IBF PROPERTY MANAGEMENT (IBFPM) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy governs data collection and usage for our website, ibfpropertymgmt.com, and our digital marketing services. By using our website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Last updated: February 07, 2025

Information Collection

When you browse the website, you do so anonymously. Personal identifiable information is not collected. Some areas of our website, however, may request that you voluntarily supply us with personal identifiable information including your name, email address, and phone number to service your inquiry.

IBFPM automatically collects usage information including geolocation, browser type, access times, and referring websites. This information is used by IBFPM to maintain quality of service and general performance statistics. No personal information is referenced.

Information Used

IBFPM collects and uses your personal information to deliver the services and or information you have requested. We may also use your personal information to inform you of other products or services available from IBFPM and its affiliates. We may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services.

IBFPM keeps track of the websites and pages you visit within our websites, in order to determine what services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within IBFPM to users whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

IBFPM does not sell, rent or transmit personal information to other organizations. IBFPM will not share your SMS consent, messages or phone numbers to other organizations for marketing purposes.

SMS Terms of Service

By opting into SMS from a web form or other medium, you are agreeing to receive SMS messages from IBF PROPERTY MANAGEMENT (IBFPM). This includes SMS messages for appointment scheduling, appointment reminders, post-visit instructions, appointment notifications, and billing notifications. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. See privacy policy https://ibfpropertymgmt.com/privacy-policy. Message HELP for help. Reply STOP to any message to opt out.

Cookies

IBFPM website uses cookies to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a small file, stored on a user's hard drive by a website. Its purpose is to collect data relating to the user's browsing habits. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. Declining cookies may decrease the quality of your user experience.

Disclosure

IBFPM considers the individual information we maintain to be confidential, our policy is to disclose no personal information gathered online to third parties unless release is required by law or is pertinent to judicial or government investigations or proceedings.

Third Party Websites

The IBFPM website may contain links to other sites, and we make every effort to link only to sites that share our high standards and respect for privacy. However, we are not responsible for the content, or the privacy practices employed by other sites.

Changes to This Statement

IBFPM will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Our company encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how we are protecting your information. When such a change is made, we will update the “Last Updated” date above.

Unsubscribe From IBFPM

You may unsubscribe from IBFPM marketing communications by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link located on the bottom of the company’s emails or responding “stop” for text messaging. Additionally, you may unsubscribe by contacting IBFPM at ayman@ibfpropertymgmt.com.
